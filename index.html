Our Story

At Comfort Zone Pub, our story begins with you. From the moment you walk through our doors, the goal has always been to give you a place where you can relax, feel at home, and share good times with the people who matter most.

When Mr. Kumar first opened the pub, his dream was to create more than just a spot for drinks, he wanted a true comfort zone where you could enjoy tasty braaied meat, a cold beer, a game of snooker, or just laughter with friends.

Like many businesses, we had to close during the COVID-19 pandemic, but thanks to your support and a fresh new look, we reopened early last year, stronger than ever. Today, Comfort Zone Pub continues to be your space to unwind, celebrate, and make lasting memories.
